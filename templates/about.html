About

Public Transport Delay Prediction for Karnataka (primary focus Bengaluru). We estimate delays and ETAs for Bus, Metro, Train using live routing, traffic heuristics and weather influence.

What’s included

  • Only shows modes that are actually available between your Source and Destination
  • Bengaluru-first geocoding (auto fallbacks to Karnataka, India)
  • Road route via TomTom for bus path; metro/train estimated with corridor speeds
  • Weather & traffic impact applied to delays

Scope

  • Coverage: Karnataka
  • Primary City: Bengaluru (Metro supported only within metro city)

How to use

  1. Go to Plan Trip, type places (e.g., “Majestic, Bengaluru”, “Indiranagar Metro Station”)
  2. Submit to view available modes, delays, ETAs, fare and map
  3. Open Dashboard for interactive summaries
